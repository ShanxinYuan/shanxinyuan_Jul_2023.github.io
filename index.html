Shanxin Yuan

I am a Lecturer in Digital Environment at School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Queen Mary University of London. Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist in Computer Vision at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab, London Research Center, UK.   I received the PhD degree from Imperial College London. My research interests are machine learning and computer vision, particularly 3D vision and low-level vision. I am an Associate Editor for The Visual Computer journal. I regularly review for major computer vision conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, and NeurIPS) and related journals (TPAMI, IJCV and TIP).
